Different Cat Breeds =^..^=

Birman Cat
  • Can live up to 15 + years.
  • Birman cats do not reach full maturity until they are 3 years old
  • The Birman was the sacred cat of Burman, believed to be the companions of the priests of the temple
    • British Shorthair Cat
  • Can live up to 12-17 years
  • Two of the most famous fictional cats are British Shorthairs: Puss in Boots and Chesire Cat from Alice in Wonderland
  • As the oldest breed in England, the British Shorthairs are thought to have descended from domestic cats imported from Egypt
    		•
    Chartreux Cat
  • Often called the smiling cat of France
  • This is a slow-maturing breed that reaches adulthood in three to five years
    		•
    Ragamuffin Cat
  • This healthy breed reaches adulthood at 4 years old.
  • easygoing, calm temperament and can be trained to walk on a leash, play fetch and sit up to beg.
    		•
    Bombay Cat
  • Can live up to 12 to 16 years
  • Many Bombays are entirely black, including their paw pads, which isn’t a common trait in other black cats.
